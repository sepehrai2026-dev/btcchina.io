Guide

Navigating crypto in China

The regulatory landscape

The PBoC banned financial institutions from providing crypto services and all domestic exchanges were shut down in 2021. However, holding cryptocurrency as personal property is not explicitly illegal. The legal grey area means users should proceed with caution.

P2P trading

Peer-to-peer platforms remain the primary on-ramp for Chinese users. These platforms connect buyers and sellers directly, with the platform providing escrow. Payment is typically via WeChat Pay, Alipay, or bank transfer.

Risk considerations

Using VPNs to access offshore exchanges carries legal risk. Bank accounts linked to frequent crypto P2P transactions may be frozen temporarily for investigation. Keep transaction amounts reasonable and maintain clean records.
